## Overview

PNP Capital Ventures LLC needed a next-generation rideshare platform to compete in the market with a focus on transparency, comfort, and safety.

## Challenge

Building a rideshare platform requires two separate but interconnected apps — one for riders and one for drivers — each with real-time location tracking, payment processing, and safety features. Both needed to be published on iOS and Android simultaneously.

## What I Did

- **Built two Flutter applications** — REVO Rideshare (rider) and REVO Rideshare Driver — sharing a common codebase architecture
- **Integrated Google Maps** for real-time GPS tracking, route calculation, and ETA estimation
- **Implemented transparent pricing** with no hidden fees, showing fare breakdowns before ride confirmation
- **Added comfort and safety features** including personalized ride settings, driver background check indicators, and in-app emergency support
- **Managed App Store and Play Store submissions** for all four app listings (2 iOS + 2 Android)

## Results

- Successfully launched 4 apps across App Store and Google Play
- Delivered a complete rideshare experience with real-time tracking and transparent pricing
- Built a rewards and referral system to drive user acquisition
