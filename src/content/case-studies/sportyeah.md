## Overview

SportYeah set out to build the largest sports community — a social network dedicated exclusively to sports enthusiasts, connecting athletes, professionals, fans, clubs, foundations, and federations.

## Challenge

Creating a sports social network required a rich feature set comparable to mainstream social platforms but tailored to the sports ecosystem: real-time scores, multi-sport news aggregation, community forums, and a monetization model for content creators.

## What I Did

- **Built the Flutter mobile app** for iOS and Android with a unified codebase
- **Implemented a multi-source news feed** aggregating sports news from different media outlets across all sports
- **Integrated live scores** for various sports with real-time updates
- **Developed community features** including user-created forums, direct chat, group messaging, and 24-hour stories
- **Built a creator monetization system** rewarding content creators who reach engagement thresholds with additional income

## Results

- Launched a comprehensive sports social network on both app stores
- Delivered a platform combining news, live scores, social features, and monetization in a single app
- Enabled community-driven content with user-managed forums and creator rewards
