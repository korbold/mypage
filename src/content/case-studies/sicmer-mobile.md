## Overview

Corporación Favorita, Ecuador's largest retail conglomerate, needed a mobile solution to manage the personnel authorized to deliver products purchased at their establishments.

## Challenge

The existing process for assigning and tracking delivery personnel lacked a proper digital workflow. Store managers had limited visibility into who was authorized, and coordination was handled through manual processes that were slow and error-prone.

## What I Did

- **Developed the SICMER Flutter app** for Android, enabling digital assignment and tracking of authorized delivery personnel
- **Integrated REST APIs** for real-time data synchronization between the mobile app and backend systems
- **Implemented Firebase services** for push notifications and crash reporting
- **Built an intuitive UI** for store managers to quickly assign and manage delivery staff

## Results

- Digitized the delivery personnel assignment workflow across Corporación Favorita stores
- Improved visibility and traceability for authorized delivery staff
- Reduced manual coordination overhead with real-time synchronization
